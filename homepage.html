Who I am and what I know

Born and raised in the vibrant landscapes of South Florida, I have ventured into the realm of programming and have been exploring this fascinating field for over a year now.
With experience spanning over 1,000 dedicated hours, I am adept at leveraging Python, C++, and Java, with additional proficiency in HTML and CSS to create user-centric solutions.
My core strengths lie in automating processes using Python, constructing purposeful programs with C++ and Java, and creating intuitive web applications through a blend of Python, HTML, and CSS.
I am proud to have made significant contributions to law firms by creating valuable software tools. My most notable projects include an interactive PDF program capable of streamlining the handling of
documents and a versatile bulk renaming tool, both tailored to enhance efficiency. Moreover, the foundation of my work philosophy is that I tailor code in a way to pioneer success for my clients.
Being the son of an IT entrepreneur, I've spent two enriching years assisting in my father's IT business, Aramistech, where I honed my skills and gained practical insights into the world of technology.
With the dream of seeing numerous businesses benefit from programs designed by me, I am driven to create robust software solutions that address diverse business needs.
Guided by my passion and inspired by my future aspirations, I am on a continuous journey to innovate, create, and lead in this ever-evolving world of technology.
